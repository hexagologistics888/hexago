Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information

Last Updated: 29 March 2026  |  Effective Date: 29 March 2026  |  Governed by: Information Technology Act, 2000 (India) & IT (Amendment) Act, 2008

Hexago Logistics and Transport ("Hexago", "we", "us", or "our") operates the website hexagologistics.in. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or contact us for logistics services.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the website.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you fill out our contact form or reach out via WhatsApp, phone, or email, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone / mobile number
  • Company name and designation
  • Nature of logistics inquiry (service type, shipment details)
  • Any messages or attachments you send us

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, standard server and browser data may be collected, including:

  • IP address and approximate location (city/region level)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs
  • Date and time of visits

We do not use advertising trackers or cross-site cookies. We do not install any tracking cookies beyond what is strictly necessary for website function.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your logistics inquiries and provide service quotes
  • To communicate with you about your shipment or business requirements
  • To improve our website usability and content
  • To send service-related updates if you opt in
  • To comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations

We do not use your personal data for automated decision-making or profiling.

3. Contact Form & Third-Party Processors

Our website contact form is powered by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form submission service. When you submit the contact form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers and then forwarded to our business email. Formspree's own Privacy Policy governs how they handle submitted data. We encourage you to review it at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

4. Cookies

Our website uses only essential, non-tracking cookies:

  • Bootstrap (CDN): No cookies set. Stylesheet and script files are loaded from the jsDelivr CDN.
  • Google Fonts: Google may set a short-lived browser cookie to serve fonts. No personal data is associated.
  • Google Maps embed: The embedded map on our Contact page may set a cookie from Google. You can disable it in your browser settings.
  • Service Worker Cache: We use a browser-side Service Worker to cache static assets for faster load times. This cache stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

We do not use advertising, analytics, or marketing cookies. You can clear cookies at any time through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website embeds or links to the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps — embedded map iframe on the Contact page. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Bootstrap 5 via jsDelivr CDN — CSS/JS assets loaded from cdn.jsdelivr.net.
  • GSAP via Cloudflare CDN — Animation library. No data collected.
  • WhatsApp Business Link — Clicking our WhatsApp button opens WhatsApp and is subject to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. We recommend reviewing their respective policies.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service delivery: With our internal operations team to process your logistics request.
  • Legal compliance: If required by law, court order, or a competent Indian government authority.
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets, with notice provided to you.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose it was collected for — typically up to 12 months for contact form inquiries. After this period, data is securely deleted or anonymized. You may request early deletion at any time (see Section 9).

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS (TLS/SSL) encryption for all website traffic
  • Strict Content Security Policy (CSP) headers
  • X-Content-Type-Options and XSS protection headers
  • Limited access to submitted form data (only authorized personnel)

No transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Rights

Under Indian data protection principles and the IT Act 2000, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for us to process your data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 11. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately for deletion.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
