Everything you should know about Hexago

Clear answers about our name, ownership, location, and what makes Hexago Logistics different from other logistics companies.

Is Hexago the same as Hexagon? +

No. Hexago Logistics and Transport is an independent Indian logistics company based in Aluva, Kerala. We are not affiliated with Hexagon AB or any other Hexagon-branded global company. The name Hexago is inspired by the hexagon shape — representing our six core pillars: Speed, Safety, Reliability, Transparency, Technology, and Customer Trust.

How do you spell Hexago Logistics? +

The correct spellings are Hexago Logistics and Transport or Hexa-go Logistics and Transport (with hyphen). Common misspellings we often see include: Hexagon Logistics, Hexa go, Hexago Logistic, Hexa-go Transport, Hexagologistics, and Hexa Go Logistics. All of these refer to the same company headquartered in Aluva, Ernakulam, Kerala.

What does Hexago mean? +

Hexago is derived from hexagon — a six-sided geometric shape known in nature and engineering for its structural strength and efficiency. The name reflects our six operational pillars that define every shipment we handle: Speed, Safety, Reliability, Transparency, Technology, and Customer Trust.

Where is Hexago Logistics located? +

Our head office is located at Vadakumury Building, Metro Pillar 174, Aluva, Ernakulam, Kerala — 683101, India. We operate Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. For enquiries, reach us at +91 70342 98745 or +91 88487 76836.

Who owns Hexago Logistics? +

Hexago Logistics and Transport is co-founded and owned by Mr. Harisankar U (Founder & Owner) and Mr. Akshay Raj K (CEO & Co-founder). The company was established in August 2024 and is privately held, headquartered in Aluva, Ernakulam, Kerala, India.

Is Hexa-go the same as Hexago? +

Yes — Hexa-go and Hexago refer to the same company: Hexago Logistics and Transport, based in Aluva, Kerala. Both spellings are used interchangeably. Our Google Business Profile uses Hexago (without hyphen) while our brand identity also appears as Hexa-go across certain listings.
