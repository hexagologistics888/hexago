The Brand Story

Meet Hexago — One Brand, Many Spellings

You may have typed Hexa, Hexago, Hexa-go, or even Hexagon Logistics — and landed right here. That's because customers across Kerala and India search for us in different ways, and every one of those names refers to the same company: Hexa-go Logistics and Transport, headquartered in Aluva, Kerala.

Where the name Hexago comes from

The name Hexago is built from two ideas. "Hexa" is the Greek root for six — the number of sides on a hexagon, one of the strongest and most efficient shapes in nature. "Go" stands for motion, movement, delivery. Put them together and you get a brand built for logistics: a six-sided structure that keeps moving.

That's also why some people write it as Hexa-go (with a hyphen), Hexa go (two words), or shorten it to Hexa. A few customers even search for "Hexagon Logistics" — a common misspelling that still lands them on our site. Whichever way you type it, we're the same team delivering across India from our Aluva, Kerala base.
