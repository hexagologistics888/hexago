Why Work at Hexa-go

Build your career in one of Kerala's fastest-growing logistics companies.

Hexa-go Logistics and Transport is expanding its operations across India — and we're looking for driven, detail-oriented individuals to join our team. Whether you're an experienced logistics professional or just starting out, we offer a hands-on work environment where your contributions directly impact business outcomes.

Our team manages FTL and PTL freight, 3PL warehousing operations, route planning, and client coordination from our base in Aluva, Ernakulam. You'll work alongside industry practitioners solving real supply chain problems every day — from dispatch optimisation to last-mile execution.

We value initiative, reliability, and clear communication. At Hexa-go, you won't be buried in hierarchy — you'll have direct access to leadership, room to grow, and the opportunity to shape how logistics works for businesses across Kerala and beyond.
